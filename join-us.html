Process

How to apply

Prospective graduate students and research candidates should prepare the following materials and send them by email to the relevant principal investigator:

  • A current curriculum vitae
  • A brief statement of research interests (1–2 pages)
  • Academic transcripts (unofficial copies are acceptable at the initial stage)
  • Names and contact details of two academic referees

For positions in the Laboratory of Food Science and Nutraceutical, please address your application to Prof. Dwaipayan Sikdar. For positions in the Laboratory of Applied Biosciences, please address your application to Assoc. Prof. Suman Mojumder. Contact details are available on the Contact page.

We aim to acknowledge all applications within two weeks. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview, which may be conducted in person or online.
