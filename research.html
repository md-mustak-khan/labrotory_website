Research

Combined research themes, active projects, and funded grants across CNAB's two laboratories.

Overview

A shared research agenda

CNAB's research spans the full spectrum from field and bench work in food chemistry and phytochemistry to computational analyses of genomes and microbial systems. What unites our work is a commitment to rigorous methodology, open science, and research questions with tangible relevance to human health and the environment in Bangladesh and the wider region.
